Welcome to Prabhawati VidyaPeeth

A truly Remarkable school

Among the top and best Schools in Ballia UP, Prabhawati VidyaPeeth has a real sense of responsibility to making young minds resilient, motivated and resourceful. Founded by Anu in 1999, Prabhawati VidyaPeeth is rooted in tradition though, makes the student community thrive with the ever-changing world and grow within to create a global impact.

An School celebrates differences in culture, countries, and personalities where students are exposed to a extra curricular activities, an immersive learning process, and holistic growth within a supportive and diverse community, that prepares them for a promising future. One of the top schools with standards in India is situated in Ballia, is Prabhawati VidyaPeeth.A school that enable students to explore their talents, broaden their vision and sharpen their focus to emerge as future leaders of tomorrow.
